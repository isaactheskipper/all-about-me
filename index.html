Who is The Skipper?

Background

The Skipper was born in a county called Bomet, and by the age of 2 years, he moved to a county called Kericho to live with his family in a tea company. He spent his primary and secondary school years in Kericho. He knew he was special by the way he could find inspiration in things that no one else could see. Later he went to university in Nairobi, where he found his true identity and started to use his gift in creating art through memories.

Why The Skipper

'I look at my life as a boat cruising in the ocean, there is no real destination but there is a journey. I believe that every boat in the ship has a commander, and the commander for my boat is me. That is why I choose to go with the skipper.'

Challenges and obstacles

The skipper narrates the various issues he faced in creating his dream, such as a lack of self-motivation, lack of approval from people around him, lack of finances to better his equipment, and a lack of a competent environment. 'I always like things to be perfect, but now as I grow older I realize that perfection is the enemy of progress.'
